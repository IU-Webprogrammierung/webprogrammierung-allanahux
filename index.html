Hi there!

I’m Allanah, 26, originally from the north of Germany near Hamburg. In 2022 I moved to Mannheim to be with my partner, and over time I discovered a new sense of home in the south.
Photography has always been part of my world. My grandfather collected cameras and passed on his love for capturing moments. As a teenager I occasionally played with his old analog models, but it wasn’t until 2024 that I truly dared to step into photography myself. Since then, it has become the way I slow down, notice details, and tell stories through images.
Design is the other path I’ve chosen. In 2022, I began studying User Experience Design, fascinated by the balance between creativity, psychology, and human needs. Today, both photography and design shape how I see the world – and how I create within it.
